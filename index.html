Wednesday

PULL + CORE
Superset = do A then B back to back with no rest, rest 90s between rounds.
SUPERSET 1 — 3 ROUNDS · rest 90s between rounds
💪
Pull-ups (or Lat Pulldown)
3 sets5–8 reps
▼
FORM CUES

Dead hang at the bottom — full extension every rep. Pull shoulder blades down and together first.

🔄
Push-ups to failure
3 setsTo failure
▼
WHY THIS PAIRS WITH PULL-UPS

Climbing is almost entirely pulling. Without antagonist pressing work, you'll develop muscular imbalances.

SUPERSET 2 — 3 ROUNDS · rest 90s between rounds
🏋️
Single-arm Dumbbell Row
3 sets8–10 reps
▼

Row the dumbbell toward your hip — think "elbow past your back pocket."

🔼
Tricep Dips or Dumbbell Overhead Press
3 sets8–10 reps
▼

Antagonist pushing muscles. Dips are preferred for climbers as they strengthen shoulder stabilizers.

CORE CIRCUIT — 2 ROUNDS
🪝
Dead Hang
2 sets20–30 seconds
▼

Builds grip strength and finger tendon resilience passively.

🦵
Hanging Leg Raises
2 sets8–10 reps
▼

Essential for overhanging routes and high feet.

🫙
Hollow Body Hold
2 sets20–30 seconds
▼

Friday

ANTAGONIST + LEGS
SUPERSET 1 — 3 ROUNDS · rest 90s between rounds
🫁
Scapular Pull-ups
3 sets8–12 reps
▼

Hang with dead-straight arms. Pull shoulder blades down and together. Prevents rotator cuff injuries.

↔️
Band Pull-Aparts
3 sets15 reps
▼

Squeeze shoulder blades together. Non-negotiable for shoulder health.

SUPERSET 2 — 3 ROUNDS · rest 90s between rounds
🦵
Goblet Squat
3 sets10–12 reps
▼

Strong quads let you actually USE high feet instead of hanging off your arms.

⚖️
Single-leg RDL
3 sets8 reps each leg
▼

You almost never stand on two feet simultaneously while climbing. Builds necessary stability.

CORE CIRCUIT — 2 ROUNDS
🚲
Bicycle Crunches
2 sets12–20 reps
▼

Obliques for twisting moves and balance on uneven terrain.

🗿
Plank
2 sets45–60 seconds
▼

Full body tension. Don't let hips sag.
